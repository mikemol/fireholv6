You can get support about sanewall using the following services:

  • Web Forums
     
    • Help,
      help about sanewall configuration directives and the services supported.
       
    • Open Discussion,
      opinions regarding sanewall, its past and its future, but also regarding iptables firewalling.
       
    • Requests for Enhancements,
      ideas or needs that you believe they will make sanewall better, they will promote it , enhance it, or simplify it.

       

  • Web Tracking systems
     
    • Tech Support Tracking System,
      help about sanewall configuration directives and the services supported.
       
    • Bug Tracking System,
      for reporting bugs and other problems regarding sanewall.
       
    • Patch Tracking System,
      for sending your patches.

       

  • Mailing Lists
     
    • Sanewall Support,
      help about sanewall configuration directives and the services supported.
       
    • Sanewall Development,
      exchange of information between sanewall developers or want-to-be developers.

       

Finally, you can send me a note at costa@tsaousis.gr.
